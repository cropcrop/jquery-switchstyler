switchstyler()

Add/Remove css classes when something fixed is hovering your target div(s).

As simple as:

$('.top-menu li').switchstyler({
  on: '.section2, .section3',
  addClass: 'newClass1 newClass2',
  removeClass: 'oldClass'
});
